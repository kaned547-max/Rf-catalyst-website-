World Firsts

No Known Comparable Technology

Capabilities with no confirmed equivalent in any competitor, academic institution, or commercial programme.

■ World First

Six-Domain Unified Integration

First known system to integrate Flow, Field, Plasma, Catalyst, Ageing, and Control as fully coupled physics domains in a single RF plasma-catalyst design framework. No competitor currently replicates this integration.

■ World First

RF-Plasma-HEA Catalyst Co-Optimisation

First known co-optimisation of RF field geometry, non-thermal plasma generation, and high-entropy alloy catalyst formulation as a unified design space. Conventional systems optimise each domain independently.

■ World First

Predictive Catalyst Ageing Model

First known digital twin incorporating real-time catalyst ageing and fouling prediction coupled to closed-loop control adjustment. Industry standard approaches are reactive, not predictive.

■ World First

Impedance-Catalyst Feedback Architecture

First known architecture where RF impedance matching is continuously updated as a function of catalyst state and flow conditions simultaneously, enabling real-time system-wide optimisation.
