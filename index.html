REGULATORY TAILWINDS

Policy Drivers Creating Mandatory Upgrade Cycles

Converging international regulatory mandates generate structural demand across RFCA's primary and secondary market segments.

ACTIVE — 2025–2027
EURO 7
European Union emissions standard for new light and heavy vehicles. Tightens NOx limits and introduces real-world driving emissions requirements, driving aftertreatment system upgrades across OEM production lines.
ACTIVE — 2027+
EPA HEAVY-DUTY 2027+
US EPA heavy-duty vehicle emissions rule. Significant tightening of NOx standards for commercial trucks and buses. Estimated to require fleet-wide powertrain and aftertreatment system upgrades.
ACTIVE — EU
EU INNOVATION FUND
European Commission fund supporting low-carbon industrial technology deployment. Aligned with RFCA's industrial emissions and energy transition applications. Relevant for Scale-up and first-of-kind projects.
ACTIVE — US
INFLATION REDUCTION ACT
US federal clean energy investment incentives. Relevant to hydrogen processing, power-to-X, and industrial decarbonisation applications. Supports RFCA's Tier 2 market development timeline.
MANDATE — 2030+
EU E-FUELS MANDATE
European Union mandate for sustainable aviation fuel blending from 2030. Drives industrial investment in synthetic fuel production pathways, directly relevant to RFCA's power-to-X and hydrogen processing segments.
PROGRAMME — ONGOING
DoD ADVANCED PROPULSION
US Department of Defense annual advanced propulsion R&D budget. SBIR/STTR Phase I programme provides structured, funded entry path into hypersonic and advanced combustion system development.
